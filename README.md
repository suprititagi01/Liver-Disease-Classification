Objective: To provide a non-invasive, data-driven tool for the early prediction and diagnosis of liver disorders, which is crucial for timely medical intervention.

Data Source: Utilizes clinical and laboratory data (e.g., specific enzyme levels, patient demographics) to train the model.

Methodology: Employs various classification algorithms (e.g., Logistic Regression, Support Vector Machines, Decision Trees, or Deep Learning techniques) to analyze patient features and predict the diagnostic outcome.

Impact: Aims to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of preliminary liver disease screening, serving as a valuable aid for healthcare professionals.
